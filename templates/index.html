how it works

Three steps from login to action plan

01 —
Connect your Google account
Read-only OAuth access. We see Gmail headers, Drive metadata, and storage totals — never email content. One account covers all linked devices.
02 —
We calculate your digital footprint
The carbon engine maps your usage against peer-reviewed emission factors from IEA, the Shift Project, and Green Algorithms. Everything broken down by category.
03 —
Get a ranked action plan
Not generic tips — specific, quantified recommendations sorted by CO₂ saved per unit of effort. Biggest wins first, every time.
✉
Gmail analysis
Volume sent, received, spam count, and storage. Thirty days of mail translated into grams of CO₂.
☁
Drive & cloud storage
Idle data costs energy 24/7. We surface how much your Drive is costing in real emission terms.
⬡
Multi-device report
One Google account, every device. Phone and laptop combined into a single aggregated report.
↗
Ranked action plan
Recommendations sorted by grams saved. Easiest and highest-impact changes always come first.
